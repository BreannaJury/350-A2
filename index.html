Who we are

Carbon Tiptoe! is a non-profit organisation dedicated to demystifying carbon footprints. We aim to help people make sense of carbon footprints and live more sustainable lives, no matter what level of consumer engagement they are involved in.

What we do

Our mission is to provide easy-to-understand information on how much your personal lifestyle has an impact on the environment – and help you to reduce it. We provide data on how many carbon footprints the average consumer generates, and how much individual lifestyle changes can reduce these footprints.

What we offer

We offer free tools for you to get involved and create a personalized footprint report (which you can share with friends and colleagues to compare and learn from others). We also highlight large producers and businesses that have carbon emissions levels higher than NZ's average.

Volunteer

Help reduce carbon emissions by volunteering with us at a range of events, fundraisers and services. You’ll get training and can choose to volunteer on a shift basis, joining the team at different times, or a full-time position.

Initiatives

Adopt a tree: Adopt a tree is a great way to support our campaign and become part of the solution to global climate change. We need to increase the number of trees in our parks and gardens, so to help achieve this, we are asking you to adopt a tree and plant it in your local area. If you have the space, please give the tree a home.
